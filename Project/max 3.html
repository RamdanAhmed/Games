Characters

Max Payne

Story: Main Protagonist of the game. Max is a former NYPD detective who has faced tremendous personal loss and tragedy throughout his life, which drives his dark and cynical outlook. By the time of Max Payne 3, Max is living in São Paulo, Brazil, working as a private security guard for the Branco family. He is consumed by his past and struggles with addiction and depression, leading him to become deeply involved in a new violent situation.

Characters

Raul Passos

Story:A former cop and Max’s partner in São Paulo. Passos is hired to assist Max with his security job, and he provides a sense of loyalty and camaraderie to Max. However, their relationship becomes complicated as the plot unfolds, and Passos becomes a more important figure in Max’s investigation.

Characters

Fabiana Branco

Story:The wife of Rodrigo Branco, a wealthy businessman who hires Max to protect his family. Fabiana is kidnapped early in the story, and her abduction is one of the key events that sets off the chain of events in the game. Max becomes personally involved in her rescue, but the situation quickly becomes more dangerous and entangled with corruption.

Characters

Rodrigo Branco

Story:A rich and influential businessman who owns the Branco Corporation in São Paulo. He hires Max to protect his family, but Rodrigo’s involvement in corrupt dealings complicates the situation. His family’s troubles lead to Max’s further entanglement in a dangerous situation.

Characters

Victor Branco

Story:The brother of Fabiana Branco and the son of Rodrigo Branco. He plays a role in the family drama and becomes part of the larger conflict surrounding the Branco family and their criminal dealings.

Characters

Armando Becker

Story:A corrupt police officer in São Paulo who works against Max as he tries to uncover the truth about the Branco family and their enemies. Becker has his own hidden agenda, making him a significant threat to Max during the game.
