Characters

Razor Callahan

Story: Razor is the leader of a group of skilled illegal street racers in the city. He is a notorious and hated character for betraying the protagonist and stealing his iconic BMW M3 GTR after a race. Throughout the game, the player seeks revenge on Razor and tries to reclaim their reputation in the world of street racing.

Characters

Ming

Story:Ming is one of the racers on the Blacklist. He is known for his heavily modified cars and aggressive racing style. He’s a tough competitor and one of the challenging characters the player faces in the game.

Characters

Ronnie

Story:Ronnie is part of the Blacklist’s list of racers. He is known for his unpredictable and sometimes reckless racing style, and he plays a key role in the game's progression.

Characters

Vic

Story:Vic is another competitor in the Blacklist, known for his high-performance modified car. He isn’t the sharpest racer, but he remains one of the many obstacles the player must overcome to climb the ranks.

Characters

Earl

Story: Earl is one of the Blacklist racers with a very powerful and highly modified car. He is one of the challenging bosses the player needs to defeat on the way to reaching Razor.

Characters

Bull

Story:Bull is a strong and aggressive racer on the Blacklist. He has a very fast car, and defeating him becomes a key part of progressing through the game’s story.
