Mafia I

Year:2002

Story:

Mafia I is set in the 1930s in the fictional city of Lost Heaven. Tommy Angelo, initially a humble cab driver, is forced to work for the Salieri Mafia after a run-in with the family. As he rises through the ranks, he becomes involved in various criminal activities, including murder, robbery, and smuggling. However, Tommy's journey is marred by betrayal, and by the end of the story, he faces the consequences of his criminal choices. Ultimately, Tommy finds himself targeted by the family he once worked for, resulting in a tragic ending.

Mafia II

Year:2010

Story:

Mafia II is set in the 1940s and 1950s in the fictional city of Empire Bay. Vito Scaletta returns from World War II to find his family struggling financially. In order to improve his life, Vito joins the Mafia, becoming involved in smuggling, bootlegging, and other illegal activities. As he rises within the criminal world, he navigates betrayal and power struggles, particularly with his friend Joe. Ultimately, Vito's ambitions lead to a confrontation with the Mafia, and the story concludes with a sense of loss and betrayal.

Mafia III

Year:2016

Story:

Mafia III takes place in 1968 in New Bordeaux, a city inspired by New Orleans. Lincoln Clay, a Vietnam War veteran, returns home to find his adoptive family murdered by the Marcano Mafia. Seeking revenge, Lincoln embarks on a violent quest to dismantle the Marcano family and their criminal empire. The game features a more open-world structure and allows players to choose how to approach Lincoln's mission. As Lincoln eliminates the Mafia's influence in New Bordeaux, he must navigate the complexities of trust, betrayal, and loyalty, ultimately reshaping the criminal landscape of the city.

far cry 1

Year:2004

Story:

Far Cry is set on a tropical island, where Jack Carver, a boat captain, is hired to take a journalist named Valerie Constantine to the island. However, after an ambush by unknown enemies, Jack is left stranded, and Valerie is captured. Jack soon discovers that the island is home to a secret research facility run by the mad scientist Dr. Krieger, who has been conducting dangerous experiments with genetically enhanced soldiers. Throughout the game, Jack fights his way through hordes of mercenaries and mutants to rescue Valerie and uncover the dark secrets of the island. The game ends with Jack defeating Dr. Krieger, but leaving open the possibility of more threats in the future.

far cry 2

Year:2008

Story:

Far Cry 2 takes place in a war-torn African country, where the player is a hired mercenary tasked with eliminating The Jackal, an arms dealer responsible for supplying weapons to both sides of the ongoing conflict. The player travels across the country, dealing with corrupt factions, dangerous wildlife, and unstable political situations. As the story progresses, the mercenary learns that The Jackal is manipulating the situation, and the player's mission becomes more complicated. The game focuses on survival, morality, and the consequences of war. The ending is ambiguous, with the protagonist either killing The Jackal or becoming involved in a larger power struggle.
