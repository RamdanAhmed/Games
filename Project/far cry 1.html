Characters

Jack Carver

Story: A former U.S. Army Special Forces operative turned boat captain. Jack is forced to confront an unknown enemy when his boat is shot down and he is stranded on a remote island. He has to navigate the island, uncover its mysteries, and fight against both mercenaries and genetically altered creatures.

Characters

Dr. Krieger

Story: A scientist working on a project to develop genetically engineered soldiers and other experimental creations. He’s responsible for the strange mutations on the island and for the creation of the "Trigen" soldiers (mutant soldiers with enhanced abilities). His experiments are key to the central mystery of the island.

Characters

Valerie Constantine

Story:A mysterious woman who is initially a source of assistance to Jack during his mission. Valerie is later revealed to have more personal stakes in the events unfolding on the island, which adds complexity to her role in the story.

Characters

Major Richard Crowe

Story:A former military officer who is now leading the mercenary forces on the island. He serves as one of the major obstacles Jack faces during the game, often in direct opposition to him.
