Characters

Vito Scaletta

Story:Vito Scaletta is the main character in Mafia 2. He is an Italian-American immigrant who is trying to make a name for himself in the criminal world of Empire Bay during the 1940s and 1950s. Vito initially turns to crime to escape poverty and provide for his family, but as he becomes more involved in the Mafia, he realizes the cost of the lifestyle. Throughout the game, Vito works his way up the ranks of the Falcone crime family, but his story is one of betrayal, friendship, and tragedy.

Characters

Joe Barbaro

Story: Joe Barbaro is Vito’s loyal friend and fellow criminal. Joe is ambitious, reckless, and sometimes impulsive, but he has a strong bond with Vito. Joe helps Vito navigate the Mafia world and is with him through many of the game’s key moments. Their friendship is central to the story of Mafia 2, though it is eventually tested as Vito becomes more deeply entrenched in the criminal underworld.

Characters

Henry Tomasino

Story: Henry is a veteran mobster who acts as Vito’s mentor throughout much of Mafia 2. He introduces Vito to the inner workings of the Falcone crime family and plays a crucial role in Vito’s rise through the ranks. While initially appearing as a reliable figure, Henry’s decisions and actions later complicate Vito’s life.
