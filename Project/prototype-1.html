Characters

Alex Mercer

Story:The main protagonist of the game. Alex was a regular human who was subjected to a failed scientific experiment that infected him with the Blacklight virus. This virus gave him extraordinary abilities, including shape-shifting, enhanced strength, and the ability to consume other people to gain their memories and abilities. He loses his memory and embarks on a journey to discover his past while seeking revenge on those who caused his transformation.

Characters

Karen Parker

Story:A key character in the story who plays a crucial role in Alex's journey. She is a scientist involved with the Blacklight virus and has important knowledge about Alex's past. Karen is one of the few people who knows more about Alex's condition and the virus.

Characters

General Rooks

Story:A high-ranking military official who leads the efforts of the Blackwatch organization to combat the virus and stop Alex. He is one of the game's primary antagonists, as he is determined to eliminate Alex and anyone associated with the virus outbreak.

Characters

Blackwatch Organization

Story: A covert military organization tasked with containing and eliminating the Blacklight virus. They are the primary force that hunts down Alex throughout the game. They represent the military's efforts to control the outbreak and stop Alex.

Characters

Dr. Ragland

Story: A scientist involved in the experiments that led to the creation of the Blacklight virus. He played a part in Alex's transformation and has crucial information about the virus and the experiments conducted on it.
