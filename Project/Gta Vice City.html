Characters

Tommy Vercetti

Story: Tommy Vercetti is the main protagonist of GTA: Vice City. He is a former hitman for the Forelli Crime Family, originally from Liberty City. Tommy was sent to Vice City to oversee a drug deal, but things go wrong and he ends up losing both the drugs and the money. The deal turns into a massacre, and Tommy is forced to find out who betrayed him while establishing himself as a powerful figure in Vice City's underworld. Tommy is known for his ruthless and fearless nature. Throughout the game, he rises to power by taking over various criminal businesses, including drug trafficking, extortion, and more, all while dealing with various enemies and rivals. Over time, Tommy builds a criminal empire in Vice City, asserting control over key parts of the city.

Characters

Lance Vance

Story: Lance Vance is Tommy’s partner and one of the key characters in Vice City. He is a former associate of Ricardo Diaz (one of Tommy's major enemies in the story). Lance initially seeks Tommy's help to get revenge on Ricardo Diaz, who killed his brother. The two team up to take down Diaz, and Lance becomes Tommy’s right-hand man throughout much of the game Despite his loyalty to Tommy, Lance is a character marked by ambition and a desire for power. As the game progresses, Lance’s motivations and his relationship with Tommy become more complicated, and it’s revealed that Lance harbors his own ambitions to take over Tommy’s empire. His betrayal is one of the key twists in the story.

Characters

Ken Rosenberg

Story: Ken Rosenberg is a lawyer and one of Tommy's earliest allies in Vice City. He is introduced to Tommy after the failed drug deal, and he becomes a key figure in helping Tommy navigate the criminal underworld of Vice City. Ken works for Sonny Forelli, but his with Tommy grows stronger as Tommy begins building his empire. Ken is depicted as a nervous, somewhat incompetent lawyer with a knack for getting in over his head, especially when it comes to his connections with the criminal world. Despite his nervousness and anxiety, Ken proves to be loyal to Tommy, assisting him in various business ventures. He provides some comic relief, but also plays a pivotal role in the game’s development and Tommy’s rise to power.

Characters

Sonny Forelli

Story: Sonny Forelli is a powerful mob boss from Liberty City and the head of the Forelli Crime Family. He is the one who sends Tommy Vercetti to Vice City, initially as a way to help Tommy expand the family’s influence and make money from a drug deal. However, when Tommy’s drug deal goes wrong, Sonny is angered and demands Tommy pay back the money and drugs he lost. As the story progresses, Sonny’s greed and impatience lead him to make several mistakes, and his relationship with Tommy becomes strained. Sonny becomes one of Tommy’s primary antagonists, and their conflict eventually culminates in a violent showdown. Sonny’s character is the epitome of mob boss greed, arrogance, and violence

Characters

Ricardo Diaz

Story: Ricardo Diaz is a powerful and ruthless drug lord in Vice City. He is initially one of Tommy’s main enemies, controlling much of the drug trade in the city. Diaz runs his operations from his mansion and has a vast network of associates and henchmen. He is known for his paranoid, volatile nature and his love for luxury. Tommy initially works for Diaz as part of his plan to build power in Vice City, but their relationship deteriorates as Tommy begins to rise in power. After a violent power struggle, Tommy manages to eliminate Diaz and take control of his operations. Diaz’s character is central to Tommy’s rise, as he is one of the first major figures Tommy must defeat to solidify his power in Vice City

Characters

Colonel Juan Garcia Cortez

Story: Colonel Juan Garcia Cortez is a former military officer turned businessman in Vice City. He is one of Tommy’s early allies and serves as a mentor figure to Tommy. Cortez introduces Tommy to the world of high society and provides him with opportunities to expand his empire. He is an influential figure in the city's political and criminal circles, with connections to both the upper class and the underworld. Despite his charming demeanor and seemingly respectable status, Cortez is deeply involved in criminal activities, particularly smuggling and drug trafficking. He helps Tommy with various missions, but his motivations are often tied to his own ambitions. Cortez is a pivotal figure in Tommy’s journey, and his relationships with other characters serve as a stepping stone for Tommy’s rise to power.
