Characters

Lincoln Clay

Story:The main protagonist of Mafia 3. After returning from the Vietnam War, Lincoln seeks revenge against the Italian Mafia for killing his adoptive family. His quest for vengeance is a central theme of the game, and he works to build his own criminal empire in New Bordeaux.

Characters

Sammy Robinson

Story: A father figure to Lincoln, Sammy is the leader of the Black Mob in New Bordeaux. His murder is the catalyst for Lincoln’s quest for revenge. Sammy’s death drives the emotional core of the game.

Characters

Cassandra

Story:One of Lincoln's key allies in his quest for revenge. She leads the Haitian Mob in New Bordeaux and has her own personal reasons for seeking to take down the Italian Mafia. She becomes an important partner to Lincoln throughout the game.

Characters

Sal Marcano:

Story:The main antagonist of Mafia 3 and the head of the Marcano crime family. He is responsible for the murder of Lincoln’s adoptive family and is the person Lincoln seeks to destroy throughout the game.
