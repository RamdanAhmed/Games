Characters

Joseph Seed (The Father)

Story:Joseph Seed, also known as The Father, is the charismatic and manipulative leader of The Project at Eden's Gate cult. He is the central antagonist of the game, a self-proclaimed prophet who believes that he has been chosen by God to lead the world through a coming apocalypse. Joseph is a complex villain with a calm and serene demeanor, which contrasts with his violent and oppressive actions. He uses his cult to control the people of Hope County, instilling fear and devotion in his followers. As the player, you must confront Joseph and his family to liberate the county from his influence.

Characters

The Seed Family

Story: The Seed family plays a major role in maintaining control over Hope County, with each member of the family controlling one of the regions. They are all highly dangerous and fanatically loyal to their father's vision of "The Project." The members of the Seed family are:

John Seed: Joseph's younger brother, known as "The Baptist". He controls the Henbane River region. John is responsible for brainwashing and manipulating people through the use of guilt and shame, often forcing them to confess sins. He is known for his psychological tactics and is one of the first members you confront.

Jacob Seed: Joseph's older brother, known as "The Soldier". He controls the Whitetail Mountains region. Jacob is a ruthless and calculating figure who uses military tactics, training, and mind control to create a loyal army of soldiers. His methods are based on violence, discipline, and warfare. He is a direct threat to anyone who opposes the cult.

Faith Seed: Joseph's adopted sister, known as "The Siren". She controls the Henbane River region alongside John. Faith is a manipulative and seductive figure who uses mind-altering substances and hallucinogenic drugs to control people’s minds. She lures people into submission through their desires, offering them a sense of peace and false salvation.

Characters

Deputy (The Player Character)

Story:The protagonist of Far Cry 5 is a deputy sheriff who is sent to Hope County to arrest Joseph Seed. However, the situation quickly spirals out of control, and the deputy becomes the central figure in the resistance against the cult. The player's choices determine the deputy's actions and relationships, making them the key force in liberating the county. The deputy’s identity is customizable, and their background is left largely up to the player.

Characters

Nick Rye

Story:Nick Rye is a former fighter pilot and one of the main allies in Far Cry 5. He is a passionate and determined individual who becomes a key member of the resistance against The Project at Eden's Gate. Nick is a father-to-be and seeks to protect his family and the future of Hope County. He offers his flying skills to help the player take down the cult's forces, especially in air-based combat.

Characters

Mary May Fairgrave

Story: Mary May is a bar owner and another key ally in Hope County. She becomes involved in the resistance after the cult takes control of the county and kills her father. Mary May is tough, resourceful, and deeply motivated to help free Hope County from the cult’s grip. She provides vital support during the player’s missions, and her story is intertwined with the broader narrative of rebellion.

Characters

Pastor Jerome

Story: Pastor Jerome is a man of faith who opposes the cult's control of Hope County. As a former preacher who witnessed the cult's violent rise, he joins the player in their mission to fight against Joseph Seed and the Project at Eden’s Gate. Pastor Jerome provides a moral and spiritual center to the resistance, offering his wisdom and insight throughout the game

Characters

Hurk Drubman Jr.

Story:Hurk is a returning character from earlier Far Cry games, known for his over-the-top personality and chaotic sense of humor. He becomes one of the player's allies in Far Cry 5, offering his help in the fight against the cult. Hurk provides both comic relief and additional firepower, often showing up in a variety of explosive situations.

Characters

The Cultists

Story:The cultists are the loyal followers of Joseph Seed and the core enforcers of his vision. They are brainwashed, fanatical, and ready to fight to the death for their leader. They control the region of Hope County, and as the player advances through the game, they will encounter various cultists, each with different skills and abilities. Some cultists are armed and dangerous, while others are more passive, lured into submission through manipulation.
