Characters

Michael

Age: 45

Story: Michael is a former bank robber who has retired and is now living a quiet life with his family in witness protection after making a deal with the FBI. However, he struggles with a mid-life crisis, feeling bored and disconnected from his family. His past as a criminal haunts him, and he longs to return to his old life of heists. His story intertwines with Trevor and Franklin, and the plot revolves around his search for purpose and fulfillment.

Characters

Trevor Phillips

Age: 37

Story: Trevor is Michael's old friend and former partner in crime. He is a chaotic, violent, and unpredictable character living in the desert far from the city. When Trevor discovers that Michael is still alive (believing he died during a previous job), he seeks him out and re-establishes their criminal relationship. Trevor's character is a symbol of disorder, and his reckless nature leads to explosive situations. While his bond with Michael is filled with tension, together they face numerous dangers and challenges.

Characters

Franklin Clinton

Age: 25

Story: Franklin is a young man from a poor neighborhood in Los Santos. He starts his criminal career by working as a repo man, stealing cars for a local gang. He dreams of escaping his tough life and achieving something greater. Franklin meets Michael and becomes involved in a heist, quickly rising through the ranks in the criminal world. Over time, he builds a strong relationship with both Michael and Trevor, helping them with various jobs while learning more about the dangerous world of crime.

Characters

Dr. David Richards

Age: 49

Story: Dr. David Richards is a secondary character in the game. He is a therapist who works with Michael De Santa as part of his efforts to cope with the emotional fallout from his criminal past. Michael attends therapy sessions with Dr. Richards to work through his troubled family life and his internal conflicts. The character serves as an important part of Michael's narrative, reflecting his struggle with personal issues and his attempt at redemption.
