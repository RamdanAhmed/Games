Characters

James Heller

Story:Main Protagonist of the game. James Heller is a former soldier whose life is turned upside down when the Blacklight virus devastates his family. After losing his wife and daughter to the virus, Heller becomes obsessed with finding and killing Alex Mercer, the protagonist of the first game, whom he holds responsible for the outbreak. Heller is infected by the Blacklight virus, gaining similar abilities to Alex, and he uses these powers to hunt down Alex and confront the truth behind the virus.

Characters

Alex Mercer

Story:Main Antagonist (for most of the game). In Prototype 2, Alex Mercer returns, but now as a more complex character. While he was the protagonist in the first game, in the sequel he is a much darker figure. As the virus’s creator and its most powerful host, Alex continues to evolve, and his motivations and actions shift throughout the story. He becomes a central antagonist for Heller, though the relationship between the two characters is more nuanced as the plot unfolds.

Characters

The Blackwatch Organization

Story:Blackwatch is an elite military unit tasked with dealing with the virus outbreak and managing the infected. They are a major antagonist in the game, and Blackwatch troops are enemies that Heller frequently faces throughout the story. The organization’s secretive and ruthless actions are at the heart of the conflict between Heller and the authorities trying to stop the virus.

Characters

Dana Mercer

Story:She is Alex Mercer's sister, and while her role is more minor in this game compared to the first one, she still plays an important part in the narrative. Dana's relationship with Alex is key to understanding his motivations, especially in light of the events of Prototype 1.

Characters

Dr. Ragland

Story:The scientist from Prototype 1 who was involved in creating the virus. His backstory and research play a larger role in the story of Prototype 2, as his knowledge becomes crucial for Heller's understanding of how to control and potentially stop the virus.
