Characters

Vito Scaletta

Story: The main protagonist of Mafia 2. A war veteran who returns to Empire Bay and gets involved in the world of organized crime after his father is killed and his family faces financial hardship. Vito works his way through the ranks of the Mafia alongside his friend Joe.

Characters

Joe Barbaro

Story: Vito's best friend and a key character in the game. Joe is bold, brash, and always willing to take risks. He plays a major role in helping Vito navigate the criminal underworld.

Characters

Henry Tomasino

Story: A made man in the Vinci family and Vito’s mentor. He is involved in various criminal activities and serves as a guide to Vito as he works his way into the Mafia's inner circle.

Characters

Salieri (Don Salieri)

Story: The boss of the Salieri family, similar to Mafia 1. His role is slightly less prominent in Mafia 2, but he is still an influential figure in the world of organized crime.
