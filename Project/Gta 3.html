Characters

Claude

Story: Claude, the protagonist of Grand Theft Auto III, is a silent and stoic character whose backstory is shrouded in mystery. He starts the game as a criminal who is betrayed by his lover, Catalina, and left for dead after a heist. Seeking revenge, Claude works for various criminal organizations in Liberty City, including the Leone Family and the Yakuza, while trying to uncover the truth behind Catalina's betrayal. Throughout GTA III, Claude's journey is one of vengeance, as he works with or against the various factions in Liberty City, including Donald Love, Asuka Kasen, and Salvatore Leone. His eventual showdown with Catalina marks the culmination of his pursuit of justice and revenge.

Characters

Salvatore Leone

Story: Salvatore Leone is the boss of the Leone Family, one of the most powerful Mafia families in Liberty City. He is introduced in Grand Theft Auto III and also appears in Liberty City Stories. Salvatore is a no-nonsense character who runs the Leone Family with a mix of old-school Mafia tactics and ruthless determination. In GTA III, Salvatore is a key player in the game's events. He initially hires Claude to carry out various jobs for the Leone Family but later grows distrustful of him. Salvatore’s relationship with Claude is strained, and after several betrayals and power struggles, Salvatore eventually meets a tragic end, being betrayed by his own family.

Characters

Catalina

Story: Catalina is one of the main antagonists in Grand Theft Auto III and also appears in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas. She is a highly manipulative and unpredictable character, involved in various criminal activities, ranging from theft to assassination. Catalina’s relationship with Claude is central to GTA III, as she is both his lover and betrayer. In GTA III, Catalina and Claude have a romantic but toxic relationship. She first manipulates him into working together to commit crimes but then betrays him during a major heist. This betrayal leads to Claude’s pursuit of revenge, making Catalina one of the game's primary antagonists. Throughout the game, her actions are driven by greed, power, and a desire to control others.

Characters

Asuka Kasen

Story: Asuka Kasen is a member of the Yakuza and one of the key figures in Grand Theft Auto III. She is a strong, intelligent, and authoritative woman, playing a central role in the criminal underworld of Liberty City. Asuka runs the Yakuza's operations in the city, and she becomes a crucial ally to Claude (the game's protagonist). In GTA III, Asuka has a complicated relationship with Claude. She’s initially an ally, providing him with several missions, but as the plot unfolds, it becomes clear that her motivations and loyalty are more complex. She shares a mutual respect with Claude, but this respect is eventually overshadowed by betrayal and violence, typical of the world they inhabit.

Characters

Donald Love

Story: Donald Love is a wealthy businessman and influential figure in Liberty City, first introduced in Grand Theft Auto III and further explored in Liberty City Stories. He is an ambitious and ruthless individual, often involved in shady dealings. Love's character is seen as someone who values power and control, manipulating others for his personal gain. In Liberty City Stories, Donald Love gets involved with the protagonist, Toni Cipriani, and tries to get him to do various jobs for him, mostly related to his quest to expand his business empire. Throughout the story, Love's darker side becomes clear, revealing that he has no problem using people as pawns for his goals.
