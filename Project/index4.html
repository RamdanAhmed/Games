Prototype 1

Year: 2009

Story:

Prototype 1 is an action-adventure game developed by Radical Entertainment and released in 2009. The game is set in New York City, where the player controls Alex Mercer, a man who suffers from amnesia after being subjected to a failed scientific experiment that turns him into a mutated being.

About

Prototype 2

Year:2012

Story:

Prototype 2 is the direct sequel to the first game, released in 2012. In this game, players control James Heller, a former soldier who becomes infected by the same virus that transformed Alex Mercer. However, instead of gaining the same powers as Alex, James gains the ability to absorb others and take on their abilities.

Max Payne 3

Year:2012

Story:

Max Payne 3 is a third-person shooter developed by Rockstar Games and released in 2012. The game follows Max Payne, a former New York City police detective who has fallen into despair after a series of personal tragedies, including the murder of his wife and child. By the time of the third game, Max has retired and is living in Brazil, working as a private security guard.

battlefield-3

Year:2011

Story:

Battlefield 3 follows the story of Sgt. Henry "Black" Blackburn, a U.S. Marine who is interrogated by the CIA about a series of terrorist attacks that have taken place in the Middle East. Through flashbacks, the story unfolds as Blackburn and his squad try to thwart a global threat led by the Russian terrorist mastermind Solomon. The narrative explores the complexities of modern warfare, military operations, and the international political tensions between the U.S. and Russia.
