Characters

Sgt. Henry "Black" Blackburn

Story:Main Protagonist of the campaign. He is a U.S. Marine who is interrogated by the CIA in the game’s opening, and his story is told through a series of flashbacks. Blackburn is a key character in investigating a terrorist plot, and he works with a squad to thwart the threats posed by a global conflict. He plays a central role in the unfolding events throughout the campaign.

Characters

Dima (Dmitri "Dima" Mayakovsky)

Story:A Russian soldier who is one of the game's main characters. Dima is involved in a covert operation alongside Blackburn. He has his own moral compass and perspective on the events that unfold, often providing a more complex view of the conflict. Dima's character is central to the game's exploration of the Russian conflict with the United States.

CIA Agent Kovic

Story:The protagonist of Far Cry 5 is a deputy sheriff who is sent to Hope County to arrest Joseph Seed. However, the situation quickly spirals out of control, and the deputy becomes the central figure in the resistance against the cult. The player's choices determine the deputy's actions and relationships, making them the key force in liberating the county. The deputy’s identity is customizable, and their background is left largely up to the player.

Characters

Solomon

Story:The primary antagonist in Battlefield 3. He is a terrorist mastermind behind the conflict that the protagonists are trying to stop. Solomon’s actions create global instability, and the main characters are tasked with preventing further chaos caused by his plans.
