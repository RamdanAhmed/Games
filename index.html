GTA 3

Year: 2001

Location: Liberty City

Story:

The protagonist in this game is Claude, a criminal who is betrayed by his girlfriend Catalina during a robbery. After being left for dead, Claude seeks revenge and gets involved with various criminal factions in Liberty City, including street gangs and the Italian mafia. Throughout the game, he works his way up through the city's underworld, engaging in violent and illegal activities, ultimately seeking power and revenge for the betrayal.

Gta Vice City

Year: 2002

Location: Vice City (a fictional city inspired by 1980s Miami)

Story:

The protagonist in this game is Tommy Vercetti, a mobster from New York who is released from prison and sent to Vice City. After a botched drug deal, Tommy finds himself drawn into a battle for control of the city's criminal underworld. Over time, he builds his own criminal empire, facing off against drug cartels, rival gangs, and corrupt officials. The game is heavily inspired by the 1980s, with a focus on themes of power, betrayal, and the rise to dominance in a city filled with excess, crime, and neon lights.

Gta San Andres

Year: 2004

Location: San Andreas (a fictional state with cities like Los Santos, San Fierro, and Las Venturas, inspired by California and Nevada)

Story:

The protagonist in this game is Carl "CJ" Johnson, who returns to his home city of Los Santos after the murder of his mother. He soon finds himself dragged back into the criminal life as he tries to restore his gang, the Grove Street Families, and seek justice for his family. The game features an extensive storyline where CJ deals with gang warfare, drug trafficking, corrupt officials, and personal betrayal. It's a story about loyalty, family, and the struggle for power in a world of crime and corruption, set across a vast and diverse landscape.

Gta IV

Year: 2008

Location: Liberty City (reimagined with a more realistic and detailed setting)

Story:

The protagonist in this game is Niko Bellic, an immigrant from Eastern Europe who comes to Liberty City in search of the "American Dream" but soon discovers that the reality is far from what he expected. Niko is chasing down a man named Roman, who betrayed his family in the past, while becoming entangled in a web of criminal activity. The game explores themes of revenge, betrayal, and moral ambiguity as Niko tries to survive in a city full of violence, corruption, and crime. It's a more dramatic and realistic entry in the series, focusing on personal conflict and the dark side of ambition.

Gta V

Year: 2013

Location: Los Santos (based on Los Angeles)

Story:

This game features three main protagonists: Michael De Santa, a former criminal who has retired but finds himself back in the criminal world due to family issues. Trevor Philips, a former friend of Michael's who is unpredictable, violent, and living in the wilderness. Franklin Clinton, a young man looking to rise above his life in the streets and join the world of organized crime. These three characters are brought together through heists, crimes, and personal conflicts, with the story revolving around stealing, betrayal, and seeking personal gain in the sprawling city of Los Santos. The game features a vast open world, multiple character perspectives, and a focus on crime, ambition, and betrayal.
